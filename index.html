Services

Our Services

Visiting Hours

• Visiting hours are from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM. Visitors must register at the reception and provide valid identification.
• No visitors are allowed beyond common areas. Overnight visitors are strictly prohibited.

Meals and Dining

• Meals are served at the designated dining area during specified hours. Residents must adhere to meal timings.
• Cooking inside the rooms is strictly prohibited. The use of electric stoves or heaters in rooms is not allowed due to safety reasons.

Laundry and Washing

• Laundry facilities are available at designated times. Residents must adhere to the laundry schedule and avoid washing clothes inside their rooms.
• Personal laundry should be done in the areas provided and should not be hung in public areas.

Cleanliness and Hygiene

• Common areas such as the dining room, bathrooms, and corridors must be kept clean at all times.
• Personal hygiene must be maintained, and residents are required to dispose of waste in the proper bins provided.
